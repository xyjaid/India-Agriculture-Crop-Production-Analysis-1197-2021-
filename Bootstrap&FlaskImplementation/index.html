Welcome

The "India Agriculture Crop Production Analysis" project is a comprehensive study that has been conducted from "1997 - 2021" which aims to analyze the crop production trends and patterns in the agricultural sector of India over a span of 25 years. The project focuses on understanding the dynamics of crop production, examining factors such as yields, productivity, and changes in cropping patterns.
